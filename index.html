About

No-nonsense manufacturing, Perth-based.

Anvil Nutrition is a contract manufacturer for supplement, sports nutrition and cosmetic brands — built to give Australian brand owners a local alternative to oversized minimums and offshore lead times.

Why we exist

Most small and mid-size Australian brands are quoted minimums of 5,000 units or more, often from manufacturers a continent — or an ocean — away. That makes testing a product, launching a line, or scaling carefully far harder than it should be.

We built Anvil to close that gap: local manufacturing in Perth, sensible minimums from 250 units, and a process you can actually follow from brief to delivery.

No buzzwords, no inflated minimums, no chasing updates across time zones. Just product, made to spec, on time.

Labels handled in-house. Design, print, and application all done at our facility — no outsourcing, no waiting on a third party. If you have artwork, we apply it. If you need it built, we build it. Every product leaves with a compliant, finished label.
